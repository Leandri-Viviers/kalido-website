Calls

Who can I call?

Only Kalido Contacts can be called through the app. To call someone you donʼt know, you must first add each other as Kalido Contacts, by exchanging names and numbers (or emails), and saving each otherʼs respective details in your phonebook.

Why do I have a monthly call limit?

Weʼd love to give you unlimited calls, but as a startup, this just isnʼt viable. We think giving all our users 60 minutes of free calls a month for their Kalido Contacts is a pretty good start though.
