Payment

Do I have to pay for Kalido?

Nope. Kalido is free for download and always will be. We believe in entrepreneurship and encourage meaningful connections, so we want as many professionals to be able to work on their own terms as possible, and as many clients to be able to find them as possible. At some later stage, premium add-ons may be charged for, but currently, the only charges will be on data from your network provider.

At the moment, youʼll both need to agree on payment outside of the app.

Sadly not. Thatʼs why we try to match you with someone you already have a trust-based connection with (common contacts, networks etc.), so youʼre less likely to meet up with someone who gives you trouble with payments. Please report unscrupulous users to us at support@kalido.me, because although we wonʼt be able to enforce payment from them, weʼll at least be able to remove them from Kalido.

The legal answer is: Youʼll have agreed to the terms of your arrangement when the work started, and any disputes will be governed by the laws of your country, or the international laws of contract and sale. The ‘people’ answer is: Talk to your service provider, youʼll probably be able to come to some sort of agreement that youʼll both be ok with.

At the moment, we donʼt. In the future, we will be introducing amazing new services that we think will be so valuable that youʼll be excited to pay for them.
