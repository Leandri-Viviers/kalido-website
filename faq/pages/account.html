My Account

Who can see my profile?

Your Kalido Contacts can see your whole profile, including all your goals. Matches will be able to see your profile, but only the goal theyʼve been matched to.

Can I change my name on Kalido?

Sure. Just go to Settings, and ‘Manage account’, and change your name.

Whatʼs a good profile picture?

One that showcases you at your best, of course. Good lighting, non-blurry, and preferably a solo shot (so no one steals your limelight).

Can I share examples of my work, or see samples of other peopleʼs work?

Sure, and we encourage you to. Thereʼs a media tab in your profile page that lets you add media. You can also link media to specific goals.

Can I provide any services on Kalido?

As long as your service is legal, itʼs welcome on Kalido.

How do I manage push notifications?

Go to your Settings and choose which notifications you want to receive.

What happens if I change my number?

You can change your number without affecting your account. Just go to Settings and ‘Manage account’.

Can I link more than one number to my account?

Yes. Just go to ‘Manage account’ under Settings and add the additional number.

Can I delete my account?

Weʼll be sorry to say goodbye, but we certainly wonʼt stop you. You can delete your account by going to ‘Manage account’ under Settings. Unfortunately, deleting your account means that all your data, including chat history, matches, goals, and media, will be lost.
