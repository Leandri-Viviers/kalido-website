Who can see my profile?

Your Kalido Contacts will be able to see your profile, including all your goals. People youʼre matched with or introduced to will see your goals.

Can I change my name on Kalido?

Sure. Just go to Settings, find your account, and change your name.

Why is my profile picture cropped in a circle?

We use landscape photos for your Match Cards (more space to showcase your good looks). But if youʼve used a profile picture that isnʼt landscape, it wonʼt be able to fill the space, so itʼll display as a cropped circle. Why not snap a new landscape profile picture from your camera phone now?

Whatʼs a good profile picture?

One that showcases you at your best, of course. Good lighting, non-blurry, and preferably a solo shot (so no one steals your limelight).

Can I share examples of my work, or see samples of other peopleʼs work?

You can add as many images as you want on your media page. You can also link these to specific goals. In the future, youʼll also be able to upload videos.

Can I provide any services on Kalido?

As long as your service is legal, itʼs welcome on Kalido.

How do I manage push notifications?

Go to your Settings and choose which notifications you want to receive.

What happens if I change my number?

At the moment, you can add a secondary number, but your primary number wonʼt be deleted. Alternatively, you can set up a second Kalido account under the new number. In the future, youʼll be able to change your number and link multiple numbers to your profile.

Can I link more than one number to my account?

Yes, you can.

Can I delete my account?

Weʼll be sorry to say goodbye, but we certainly wonʼt stop you. You can tap Settings on your profile screen and choose ‘Delete my account’. Unfortunately, deleting your account means all your data, including chat history, matches, goals, pictures, and other media will be lost.
