Privacy and Security

Why does Kalido need access to my phonebook?

Kalido uses your phonebook to generate matches for you. Matching with someone you already know generates a more trustworthy connection than someone you donʼt. It also lets you invite any of your contacts to join Kalido easily and conveniently. Remember, you can only send text and images to your general contacts from Kalido, but once youʼve added them as Kalido Contacts, youʼll be able to call them too (for 60 minutes each month, to each Kalido Contact, for free)!

Who can I message and who can message me?

You can message any of your Kalido Contacts, and anyone youʼve been matched with, or introduced to. Youʼll also be able to receive messages from these people.

Why canʼt I see the profile of some of my contacts?

It could be that theyʼre not on Kalido yet (hey, why not send them an invite?). Or it could be that they donʼt have your personal details saved in their phonebook (why not send them your details now?).

Can I block someone in my phonebook from seeing my profile?

Sure thing. Just tap ‘Block’ from your Nearby screen.

I worry about being stalked or hassled, can I block someone?

Kalido takes your security very seriously and with our built-in safety features, stalking, other unsavoury, or illegal behaviour should be very rare. We only share your personal information with your Kalido Contacts (people youʼve trusted with your personal details). Only general information about your goals is shared with other Kalido users (to match you and help you decide whether to work together). In addition, you can choose to stop sharing your location with a specific person. Just go to your Nearby screen, tap on the user, and opt to stop sharing your location with them. This person will never match with you again. In the event of bad behaviour please report it to us. Weʼll deal with it quite severely.

Why does Kalido need my location?

Kalido uses your present location to find you the best matches for your goals. However, for your safety, your location is only ever given as a general estimate, and never closer than 250m. Your Kalido Contacts will see your current country, city, or neighbourhood (whichever is most relevant based on where they are), while other users will only see relative distance from themselves.

Who can see my location?

Your Kalido Contacts will see your current country, city, or neighbourhood (whichever is most relevant based on where they are), while other users will only see relative distance from themselves. For your safety, your location is only ever given as a general estimate, and never closer than 250m.

Can I control who sees my location?

By default, your Kalido Contacts will see your current country, city, or neighbourhood (whichever is most relevant based on where they are), while other users will see relative distance from themselves. For your safety, your location is only ever given as a general estimate, and never closer than 250m. You can block a specific person from seeing your location, and you can also stop sharing your location completely. Unfortunately, Kalido wonʼt work at its best if you do the latter though, as we wonʼt be able to tell how close your matches are to you.

Iʼm travelling at the moment, but I havenʼt updated my location. How will I be matched?

Kalido picks up your current location to find great matches wherever you are now. This means youʼll find great matches that you can meet and work with, wherever in the world you may be. We donʼt allow you to manually set a location to match on, but we may introduce a feature that allows you to do this in the future, so that you can continue to match with people at your permanent location, even while youʼre on your week getaway.

How secure is Kalido?

Very. All information you give Kalido is secured on your device and our servers using transparent database encryption, AES-256 and SHA2; while in transit using SSL; and on a call using SRTP. Kalido will also never transmit your exact location, so no one can reverse engineer this on their phones.

Are you going to sell my personal information to advertisers?

No way. We wonʼt share personally identifiable information with any third parties without your express permission.
